Site Plan: Poise Enterprise

1. Site Name

Site Name: Poise Enterprise

Poise Enterprise is an online fashion store dedicated to providing stylish and high-quality clothing. The name "Poise" signifies elegance and confidence, making it the perfect brand for fashion lovers.

2. Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online shopping platform where customers can browse, select, and purchase fashionable clothing. It will feature different collections, special discounts, and a seamless checkout experience.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the latest fashion collections available on Poise Enterprise?
  • How can I place an order and track my delivery?

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: Gold (#FFD700) - Used for branding and highlights.

Secondary Color: White/Light Gray (#AAAAAA) - Used for contrast and readability.

Background: Black (Transparent Overlay) (#000000) - Gives a modern and elegant look.

Hover Color: Whitesmoke (#F5F5F5) - Used for link hover effects.

5. Typography

Headings: Montserrat

Body Text: Open Sans
